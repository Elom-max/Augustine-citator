### Public passages

The add-in ships with a bank of public-domain passages drawn from:

- **Patrologia Latina** (J.-P. Migne, 1844-1865) — public domain.
- French translations from **L. Péronne et al.** (Vivès, 1869-1878) —
  public domain.

These are safe to redistribute. References (e.g. `conf. X, 27, 38`)
are factual and not subject to copyright.
